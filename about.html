Our journey began in the beautiful city of Medan, where we discovered the rich history and culture of Gayo coffee, one of the most famous coffee from Aceh, Indonesia.


That encounter made us explored different regions, where we learned about the unique characteristics of each region's coffee, the farming practices, and the fascinating stories behind the coffee.
